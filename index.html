Why it matters

Kids are more capable
than we think.

Most cooking apps treat kids like toddlers or assume they're adults. Yum Lab is built for the 12+ kid who is ready for real independence — in the kitchen and in life.

🍳

End-to-End Kitchen Skills

From identifying what's in the fridge to plating, cleanup, and everything in between — Yum Lab teaches the full arc of real cooking, not just following a list.

🛡️

Built for Independent Kids

Every step accounts for safety — dangerous appliances get video tutorials, parents get payment notifications, and AI keeps kids guided without hand-holding.

🌍

Life Skills That Last

Cooking is the gateway to nutrition literacy, budgeting, planning, and self-sufficiency. Yum Lab plants that seed at the right age, with the right tone.

👥

A Community of Peers

Kids share dishes, earn badges, and follow chefs their own age. Social learning keeps motivation high far longer than solo app use.

🤖

AI That Works Hands-Free

Voice and image AI means kids don't have to stop chopping to tap a screen. The assistant watches, listens, and guides — just like a mentor standing nearby.

🧪

Progress That's Visible

Every recipe completed levels up a skill. Knife Skills, Heat Management, Seasoning — kids can see themselves growing, and so can their parents.

Use Case 01

Cook what's already
in your kitchen.

Open the fridge, tap "Explore Fridge," and Yum Lab figures out what you can make — right now, with zero shopping needed.

1

Scan your kitchen. AI image recognition identifies your ingredients and appliances from a quick photo.

2

Get matched recipes. Yum Lab surfaces recipes you can actually make — no missing ingredients.

3

Start the Lab. Follow guided prep, get cooking tips, use AI voice assistance hands-free, and finish with clean-up guidance.

Use Case 02

Dream recipe?
Shop just what
you need.

Kids pick any recipe, Yum Lab calculates exactly what's missing — and lets them buy just those ingredients in the right amounts. No waste. No giant grocery hauls.

1

Pick a dream recipe. Browse trending dishes, community favorites, or search anything.

2

Check what's missing. Yum Lab cross-references your pantry and shows only the gap ingredients.

3

Shop in small amounts. Buy a pinch, a cup, or just enough. Checkout sends a payment request to a parent for verification. ✅

Use Case 03

Build your skill tree.
Grow with the
community.

Every recipe completed levels up a real cooking skill. Knife work, heat management, seasoning, baking science — kids see themselves getting better, and so do their peers.

1

Complete Labs to level up. Finishing recipes earns XP toward specific skills in your tree — Knife Skills, Seasoning, Heat Management, and more.

2

Earn badges & titles. Collect achievements like "Pasta Pro," "Healthy Eater," or "Junior Scientist" as your profile grows.

3

Share your dish. Post to the community feed, react to other chefs' creations, and find inspiration from kids your age around the world.

AI-Powered Kitchen Assistant

Hands free.
Eyes on the food.

Three AI features designed so kids never have to choose between watching the stove and reading a screen.

🎙️

AI Voice Assistant

Talk to Yum Lab hands-free while cooking. Ask "what's next?", set timers, get ingredient swaps, or hear a step repeated — without touching the screen.

📸

AI Image Recognition

Point the camera at any ingredient or appliance. Yum Lab identifies it instantly — confirming what you have, flagging what's missing, and suggesting alternatives.

🎬

Safety Video Tutorials

Knife? Oven? Stand mixer? When the camera detects a dangerous appliance, Yum Lab auto-surfaces a safety tutorial specific to that exact tool — before the kid touches it.
